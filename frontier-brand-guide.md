# Typography

The Gotham type family is our corporate font. Gotham is to be used for headlines and copy. Short headlines or labels can be all caps.

## Headline Styles
- HEADLINES ARE GOTHAM BOLD IN ALL CAPS
- headlines in small caps and gotham black seem more friendly

## Body Copy
- Gotham Medium
- Automatic line spacing and character spacing

# Replacement Typography

## Montserrat
The Montserrat type family is our online equivalent of the Gotham corporate font. It is an open-source Google font and should be used for all digital applications where Gotham is not available.
